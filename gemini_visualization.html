The Herd Mentality

The most striking pattern in the sector is Directional Synchronization. When the market moves, the "Big Six" move as a single asset class.

Whether facing regulatory headwinds in 2019 or explosive growth in 2026, no single bank is immune to the sector's gravitational pull. Note the "Universal Up" years of 2022 and 2026.

Key Insight:

2026 was a "Super Cycle" year where every bank posted >29% gains.

The Anchor: National Bank

Consistency Over Volatility

While others fluctuate wildly, National Bank (NA) acts as the sector's anchor. It exhibits a unique "High-Floor" pattern, never dropping below Rank 4 in eight years.

  • ✓ Recovery Leader: Consistently Top 2 during recovery years (2020, 2022, 2024).
  • ✓ Down-Market Resilience: Lost less value than peers in bearish 2023.
